# Quick Meal Check Site Publish Checklist

- Replace every `replace-this-email@example.com` placeholder with your real support or privacy address.
- Replace the placeholder App Store URL on `site/index.html`.
- Update the privacy-policy contact section if your account-deletion flow wording changes.
- Add the public privacy-policy URL to App Store Connect.
- Add the same privacy-policy URL and support entry point inside the iOS app.
- After you implement the in-app deletion flow, update `site/privacy.html` and `site/support.html` if the user-facing steps differ.
- Review the privacy policy against your live Firebase and OpenAI data-retention settings before publishing.
