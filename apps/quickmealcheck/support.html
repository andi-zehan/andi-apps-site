FAQ

Common questions

Why do I need an account?

The app uses account sign-in to associate subscription status with your access to the paid analysis features.

Why does the app ask for camera or photo-library access?

Camera access is used to capture meal photos. Photo-library access is used only if you choose to upload an existing image.

Are analysis results medical advice?

No. Quick Meal Check provides nutrition estimates for convenience and trend review. It is not medical advice or diagnosis.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are purchased and managed through Apple. To cancel, use your iPhone's Apple subscription settings.

How many meal analyses can I use per day?

Quick Meal Check currently allows up to 50 meal analyses per account per day. The daily limit resets according to the app's UTC service day.

How do I delete my account or data?

Use the in-app account-deletion flow when it is available. If you cannot access that flow, contact qmc-support@gmail.com and include the email address tied to your Quick Meal Check account.

How long are meals kept in the app?

Recent meal history saved by the app is currently kept locally on the device for roughly 30 days unless you delete entries sooner.
